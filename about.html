About Me

Game Developer with a degree in gaming
and a passion for making fascinating experiences.

Bash Kurgunayev

I am a game developer currently based in the USA. I’m originally from a tiny little country called Azerbaijan, but I’ve been living in the US since 2015. I graduated from Shawnee State University with a degree in Digital Simulation and Game Engineering Technology. I tried my hand in enterprise development, but it didn't give me the same dopamine rush I got from seeing my work in a video game. The challenge of making video games keeps me on my toes and I love it. I’m constantly learning new things and sharing knowledge with fellow nerds. I like making things that help or entertain people. I love watching the users interact with my creation while I take notes on how I can improve the user experience.
When I’m not coding, I like being outside, kayaking, listening to audiobooks and podcasts, and hanging out with my 2 cockatiels.
